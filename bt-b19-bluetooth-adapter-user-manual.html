Step 1

Ensure that the Esinkin audio adapter* is plugged in and connected via the 3.5 mm audio jack / cinch cable to the audio system. You will have to select the “AUX” oder “Line-In” jack as a source. Do not separate the two devices for more than 3 feet while pairing.

Step 2

Press the pairing button (middle) on the unit. The LED will flash blue. The receiver is now ready for pairing and can be connected with other Bluetooth enabled devices.

Step 3

Turn on the Bluetooth mode on your mobile device. It will automatically search for other Bluetooth enabled devices nearby. Choose the “Esinkin BT Adapter” device from the list. The LED will stay solid blue. – If your mobile device requests a password, please try the combination “0000” or “8888“.

Step 4

In case the audio receiver and the mobile devices were paired previously, it’s no more required to start pairing again since both devices will automatically pair next time.

Step 5 (optional)

To pair a second device, just turn off the Bluetooth mode on the already paired (first) device. Press the pairing button and follow again the above steps 1 to 3 for the second device. After the successful pairing, turn on the Bluetooth mode on the first device and both devices are connected simultaneously. NOTE: Only one device can stream audio. The other device must pause streaming. You can setup up to 8 different devices but only 2 devices can be used in parallel.

Additional Information

The Esinkin audio adapter* cannot be turned off. You have to unplug the power cable in order to turn off the receiver.
